Manifesto: The Integrated Body

Self-mastery is not a single discipline. It is the ongoing practice of working with the whole body—the physiological, the psychological, and the perceptual—as one integrated system[cite: 8].

This is the premise The Character Archive is built on[cite: 7]. It does not begin with mindset or habits; it begins with the body's fundamental need for safety[cite: 10, 11].

A nervous system that does not feel safe cannot access executive function[cite: 11]. A gut that is chronically inflamed undermines emotional regulation[cite: 12].

The work is building the conditions under which your system can finally work with you rather than against you[cite: 18].
